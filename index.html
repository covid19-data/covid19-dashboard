Case Fatality Rate

COVID-19 Case Trend

This visualization shows the temporal evolution of national-level confirmed cases and the number of deaths, for the countries with at least one death. The visualization incorporates new case data whenever the data source gets updated. Please note that the case fatality is affected by various factors, particularly at the early stage of the outbreak. Because of strong age-correlated variance in the fatality rate, population structure of a country or the place of early clustered outbreaks may significantly affect the overall case fatality. Yet, high fatality rate may indicate too much stress to the healthcare system or the lack of aggressive testing in the country.

It is created by YY Ahn (@yy) and Sina Kianersi (@sina_kianersi) at the Indiana University Bloomington. The visualization is built on the recreation of GapMinder visualization by @mbostock.
